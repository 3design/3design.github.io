HIBALL

Incentivising long[er]-term collaboration

Welcome to the Helmholtz International BigBrain Analytics Learning Laboratory (HIBALL). Our aim is to transform the successful collaboration between McGill (MNI, BIC) and Forschungszentrum Jülich (INM-1, JSC) to the next level by reinforcing utilization and co-development of the latest AI and high-performance computing (HPC) technologies for building highly detailed 3D brain models, in close collaboration with CIFAR and MILA in Canada, and HAICU, the Helmholtz AI coordination unit.

HIBALL Goals

Multimodal Atlas
High quality parcellations of the BigBrain.
Virtual BigBrain and Simulation Platform
Expanding with AI-based 3D reconstruction of brain sections.
AI Link to NSC and Brain Inspired Computing
Generate a 3D map of functional neuroanatomy at the cellular level.
Open Community Resource
Optimize to become highly interoperable.
